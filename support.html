Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my data?

To completely reset your app data and start fresh, simply uninstall the Opti AI app from your device and reinstall it from the App Store.

Is my data synced across devices?

Currently, Opti AI stores your data locally on your device to ensure privacy and offline functionality.
