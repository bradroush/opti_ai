Privacy Policy for Opti AI

Last Updated: March 2026

Privacy Commitment: Opti AI is built with a "Local First" philosophy. Your physical profile data (age, height, weight) and food logs are stored on your device, not on our servers.

1. On-Device Data Storage

To provide personalized fitness suggestions, Opti AI stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Physical attributes (Age, Height, Weight)
  • Daily fitness goals and food logs

We do not have access to this data. It is never sold, shared, or uploaded to our servers.

2. AI Processing

Opti AI uses Artificial Intelligence (such as Google Gemini) to provide nutrition analysis and fitness advice:

  • Food Analysis: Descriptions of food items you enter are sent to the AI to retrieve nutritional facts.
  • Stateless Processing: Any physical attributes sent to the AI for the purpose of generating advice are processed in a "stateless" manner. Our AI partners do not retain this data to build a profile of you or for training purposes.

3. Anonymous Analytics & Stability

We use Google Firebase to ensure the app is stable and to understand general usage trends:

  • Crashlytics: If the app crashes, we receive an anonymous technical report to help us fix the bug.
  • Analytics: We track anonymous interactions (e.g., "User logged a meal") to improve app features. This is never linked to your personal identity or specific health data.

4. Children’s Privacy

Opti AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under 13.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Because your data is stored locally, you are in total control. Deleting the Opti AI app from your device will permanently remove all your data. We cannot recover this data for you as we never had access to it.
