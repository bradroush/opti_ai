Privacy Policy for Opti AI

Last Updated: March 2026

Privacy Commitment: Opti AI is built with a "Local First" philosophy. Your physical profile data and food logs are stored on your device, not on our servers.

1. On-Device Profile Data

To provide personalized fitness suggestions, Opti AI allows you to enter profile information such as age, height, weight, and fitness goals.
• This data is stored locally on your iPhone.
• We do not have access to this information, and it is never uploaded to our servers.

2. AI Processing & Nutrition Data

Opti AI uses Artificial Intelligence to analyze food items and generate fitness recommendations.

  • Food Analysis: When you request nutrient info, the description of the food is processed by our AI partners (such as Google Gemini) to return nutritional data.
  • Fitness Suggestions: To generate tailored workouts or advice, the AI processes the physical attributes you've provided. This processing is "stateless," meaning the AI providers do not retain this data to build a profile of you.

3. Anonymous Usage & Diagnostics

We use Google Firebase to keep the app stable and efficient:

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous interaction data (e.g., "User clicked 'Generate Workout'") to help us improve the app. This is not linked to your identity or your specific physical profile data.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Sends anonymous technical logs if the app crashes so we can provide fixes.

4. Data Control

Since all profile data is stored locally, you have total control. Deleting the app will permanently remove all of your profile information and history from your device. We cannot recover this data for you as we never possessed it.
